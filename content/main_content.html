What is Ector Grow?

Ector Grow was built for hydroponic cannabis growers as a way to manage, track and record the progress of their personal grows!

  • Monitor the complete lifecycle of your plants
  • Record notes to reference for your future grows
  • Consistenly (and easily) give your plants the proper nutrients to thrive
  • Automatically calculate harvest totals and easily see what you should grow next

How Does it Work?

ADD YOUR PLANTS

When entering your plants you can enter information like the strain you are growing, the date the seed was started, whether it is a clone or grown from seed and much more!

MONITOR THEIR GROWTH

Track their progress and make sure they are getting the right nutrients. Easily transition from one phase to the next, making sure to give the proper nutruents when needed.

MANAGE THEIR NUTRIENTS

Maintaining a consistent nutrient schedule is incredibly easy with the automated nutrient chart. Once your plants are added, they will automatically appear on the nutrient chart in their current position on the schedule.

CALCULATE & IMPROVE

One of the best parts of any grow - measuring your harvest! Easily calculate your grow totals and measure your progress. Take notes on changes you see and continue improving the quality and yields of your personal grows.
